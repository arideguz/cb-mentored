bún bò huế

description

Bún bò Huế is a rich and spicy soup with deep layers of flavor, paired with tender slices of beef and pork, then topped with lots of fresh herbs.

ingredients

  • rice vermicelli noodles
  • beef shank
  • fresh herbs
  • beef bone broth
